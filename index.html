DATA MANAGEMENT

For specific businesses requirements, Inversius can build a data model combining various data sources that fit into specific corporate requirements. Depending on specific customer case – we use Star or Snowflake Shema. Our services include: conceptual (defines relationships between the key entities), logical (describes data in as much detail as possible) and physical data modeling (defines tables, their structure, and the relationship between them; also data types for columns).

For an industry where no standard database exists, Inversius will work with a customer on your requirements to create one from scratch. Databases after implementation can be used in web or in-house applications. We are well familiar with Commercial vendor Databases (Oracle, MS SQL), OpenSource databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL) and NoSQL Databases (MongoDB, Neo4J).

Inversius services cover integration of various data resources. It is a common that companies need to combine more than one resource of data to get a “bigger” picture and gain competitive edge. Inside information from ERP system, ticketing systems, accounting, sales, CRM and other systems are combined with unstructured data (text files, excel sheets etc).

We help our clients transform data into intelligent insights combined with easy-to use interface. If you are a data-driven company – Inversius has a profound knowledge in data analytics and data visualization – building reports and dashboards for retail, manufacturing and finance industry.
